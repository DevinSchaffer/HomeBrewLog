| Type | Time | Amount |

### Post-Boil
1. Chill quickly to under 80 °F
1. Transfer to fermentation vessel
1. Agitate for 1-2 minutes
1. Pitch yeast

### Finishing
1. Wait ~7 days
1. Cold crash for 2 days
1. Add gelatin
1. Wait 2 days
1. Keg
